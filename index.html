Welcome to the Brioche's website. The Brioche is a small independent cafe located in the heart of Totnes, Devon.

Established back in the 70's by a Spanish lady called Remy, the 'Brioche' was a place where students would come and hang out. Over 50 years later it is still a very popular spot for locals and tourists alike to enjoy a bite to eat and something drink. The current owner has been running the cafe for the last 17 years or so.

Offering menus that cater to all tastes, with traditional English breakfasts that include veggie and vegan options the Brioche has something for everyone. At lunchtime why not try one of our small batch homemade soups, a freshly made toastie, choose from a selection of tasty fillings.

We believe that a great drink should accompany great food. We serve a high quality fairtrade ground coffee from Puro. We also believe that you shouldn't get a lesser coffee if you prefer a decaf drink so we serve a decaf coffee from the same maker.

If you fancy a cuppa then it's classic PG Tips or Tetley if you prefer a decaf cuppa. For our extensive range of herbal teas we always use a high quality brand such as Tea Pigs, Taylor's or similar.

We also offer a range of cold drinks including cans of Coke, Diet Coke, Tango, 7-up, Rio and others. Bottled still and sparkling water, a range of fruit juices, smoothies and milkshakes plus a selection of alcoholic beverages if you fancy a drink with your meal.

We have two menus, our breakfast menu which is available from 0900hrs until 1145hrs and our lunch menu which is available from 1145hrs until 1445hrs. Our specials board forms part of our lunch menu and usually comprises a couple of small batch homestyle soups and a jacket potato plus filling.

Our breakfast menu includes traditional English breakfasts with veggie and vegan options, scrambled egg on toast with options that include sausage, bacon, mushrooms plus others. We're very happy to cater for any dietary requirements you may have so please ask and we will do our best to accommodate you.

For lunch we offer a range of toasties, sandwiches, salads, jacket potatoes, soups and specials and of course we have a range of locally sourced cakes that make a great accompaniment to your main meal or indeed can be enjoyed on their own with a cuppa.
