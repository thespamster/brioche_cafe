Welcome to the Brioche's website. The Brioche is a small independent cafe located in the heart of Totnes, Devon.

Established back in the 70's by a French lady, hence the name 'Brioche' the cafe has been serving the local community for over 50 years. In that time it has become a popular spot for locals and tourists alike to enjoy a bite to eat, a coffee, or a piece of cake. The current owner has built on this reputation over the last 17 years.

Offering menus that cater to all tastes, with traditional English breakfasts that include veggie and vegan options the Brioche has something for everyone. At lunchtime why not try one of our small batch homemade soups or a freshly made toastie, choose from a selection of tasty fillings.

We believe that a great drink should accompany great food. We serve a high quality fairtrade ground coffee from Puro. We also believe that you shouldn't get a lesser coffee if you prefer a decaf drink so we serve a decaf coffee from the same maker.

The tea we serve is the classic PG Tips and Tetley if you prefer a decaf cuppa. For our extensive range of herbal teas we always use a high quality brand such as Tea Pigs or similar.

We also carry a range of cold drinks including cans of Coke, Diet Coke, Tango, 7-up, Rio and others. Bottled still and sparkling water. We also have a range of fruit juices and smoothies plus a selection of alcoholic beverages if you fancy a drink with your meal.
